## Overview

Get hands-on with RMFCoach using CFA-based RMF simulation.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Go to https://rmfcoach.com and log into your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select CFA module">
    On the Dashboard, pick a CFA scenario that fits your study goal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch simulation">
    Click "Start Simulation" to begin the interactive RMF process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make your decisions">
    Read scenario prompts and select appropriate NIST RMF actions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review feedback">
    Submit responses to get instant feedback and recommendations.
  </Step>
</Steps>
