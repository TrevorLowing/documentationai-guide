## Overview

RMFCoach lets you practice NIST RMF decisions using CFA documentation and scenarios.

## Steps

1. Click "Start Simulation" on your dashboard.
2. Review the CFA scenario details provided.
3. Make real NIST RMF decisions when prompted.
4. Submit your answers to get instant feedback.
5. Check results and compare with best practices.

## Tip

You can reset simulations anytime to try again.
