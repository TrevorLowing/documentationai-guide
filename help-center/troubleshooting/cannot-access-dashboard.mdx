---
title: Cannot Access Dashboard
description: Fix login and access issues in RMFCoach.
---

## Symptoms

- "Access Denied" or blank dashboard page
- Stuck at loading screen

## Causes

- Incorrect login credentials
- Session expired
- Browser cache issue

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log out and back in">
    Log out from RMFCoach completely. Then sign in again using your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Clear cache/cookies, then refresh RMFCoach.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check browser compatibility">
    Use Chrome or Firefox for best results.
  </Step>
</Steps>
