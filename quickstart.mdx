## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info">
Before you begin, make sure you have a modern web browser and an email address ready for account verification. No prior FedRAMP experience is required.
</Callout>

## Create Your Account and Sign In

Follow these steps to set up your profile and access the simulation environment.

<Steps>
<Step title="Sign up" icon="user-plus">
Navigate to the RMFCoach homepage and select the sign-up option. Provide your email address and create a secure password.
</Step>
<Step title="Verify your email" icon="mail">
Check your inbox for a verification link and click it to activate your account.
</Step>
<Step title="Sign in" icon="log-in">
Return to the login page, enter your credentials, and access the dashboard.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Select a Cloud Scenario and Configure Your System

Once signed in, choose a realistic cloud deployment to begin your authorization cycle.

<Columns cols={2}>
<Card title="Moderate Baseline" icon="shield" href="#moderate">
Start with a standard FedRAMP Moderate scenario that includes common NIST SP 800-53 Rev 5 controls.
</Card>
<Card title="High Baseline" icon="lock" href="#high">
Select an advanced scenario with stricter control requirements for high-impact systems.
</Card>
</Columns>

## Navigate the Three Simulation Phases

The simulation follows the core NIST RMF workflow across three interactive phases.

<Tabs>
<Tab title="Phase 1: Configure" icon="settings">
Review the system primer, select your cloud provider, and identify inherited versus customer-owned controls.
</Tab>
<Tab title="Phase 2: Implement" icon="file-text">
Write SSP narratives for selected controls such as AC-2, AU-6, and SC-7 using guided prompts.
</Tab>
<Tab title="Phase 3: Assess" icon="check-circle">
Respond to assessor findings, update the Customer Responsibility Matrix, and prepare the authorization package.
</Tab>
</Tabs>

## Complete Your First Practice Run

Finish a full cycle to build confidence before tackling more complex scenarios.

<Steps>
<Step title="Make control decisions" icon="list">
Evaluate each control family and record implementation details in the interface.
</Step>
<Step title="Run the assessment" icon="play">
Submit your package for simulated review and receive feedback on narrative quality.
</Step>
<Step title="Review results" icon="bar-chart">
Analyze your authorization decision and export the completed artifacts for study.
</Step>
</Steps>
